The Grand Chair represents our most expensive seating option, crafted entirely from recycled and reclaimed materials. This premium piece demonstrates that luxury and environmental responsibility can coexist. Every component has been carefully sourced from recycled sources and assembled with traditional craftsmanship techniques.

Key Features

  • 100% recycled materials throughout
  • Reclaimed hardwood frame
  • Recycled ocean plastic upholstery
  • Carbon-neutral production process
  • Lifetime warranty on frame
  • Plant a tree with every purchase

Environmental Commitment

The Grand Chair is our flagship sustainable product. The higher price point reflects the extensive work required to source, process, and craft furniture entirely from recycled materials. Each chair diverts approximately 45 pounds of waste from landfills and represents our commitment to circular economy principles. The reclaimed hardwood frame comes from deconstructed buildings, while the upholstery fabric is woven from recycled ocean plastics collected through coastal cleanup initiatives.
