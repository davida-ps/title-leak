Top 5 Language Learning Strategies

  1. Immerse yourself in native content daily
  2. Practice speaking from day one
  3. Use spaced repetition for vocabulary
  4. Find a language exchange partner
  5. Set specific, measurable goals

Learning a new language opens doors to different cultures, career opportunities, and personal growth. The key is consistency and finding methods that work for your learning style.
